## KS State-Specific Notes

- Kansas requires 18 CE hours per 2-year renewal period with no mandatory classroom/self-study split. This manifest delivers ~17.91 hrs, which rounds to 18 hrs and satisfies the requirement.
- The classroom-equivalent courses (ETH + LHOPT + PCPER) are structured for quality but are not required to be classroom-equivalent under KS rules.
- No carryover of excess CE hours is permitted in Kansas.
- Annuity training: 1x 4-hour course required — not covered by this catalog; must be sourced separately or built as a standalone course.
- Flood insurance: 1x 3-hour course required for P&C licensees — not covered by this catalog; must be sourced separately.
- No proctored exam required for self-study delivery.
- If the state requires exactly 18 hours (no rounding), add PCPER L3 Ch3 (0.62 hrs) to bring total to ~18.53 hrs. Confirm rounding rules with KS Insurance Department before filing.
