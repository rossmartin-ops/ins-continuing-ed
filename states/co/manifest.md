# CO Content Manifest

State: Colorado
Tier: 4 (Blocked — missing state-specific content)
Requirement: 24 CE hours per 2-year license term
- Ethics: 3 hours minimum
- Homeowners CE: 3 hours (mandatory each cycle for P/C and Personal Lines producers, state-specific)
- Self-study split: No formal split requirement
- Proctored exam: Not required
- Carryover: Up to 12 hrs to next renewal period

LTC requirements:
- LTC: 16-hr initial (partial classroom required) + 5-hr ongoing per renewal
- Note: Partial classroom requirement for initial LTC training — some hours must be live/classroom format

Specialty requirements (one-time):
- Annuity: 4 hrs (1-time)

Launch status: BLOCKED for P/C and Personal Lines producers pending CO Homeowners Insurance CE course (3 hrs, mandatory each cycle).
L/H-only producers may be launchable (no mandatory P/C-specific course requirement). Confirm with CO DOI.
Available content (TX baseline, 23.87 hrs) is built out below. See MISSING CONTENT section at the end.

## ⚠️ MISSING CONTENT — BLOCKED

### What is missing:

**CO.INS-CE-HO — Colorado Homeowners Insurance CE (3 hrs)**
- Mandatory each renewal cycle for P/C and Personal Lines licensed producers
- Must cover: Colorado-specific homeowners insurance topics, CO DOI homeowners coverage requirements, Colorado property insurance issues (hail damage, wildfire, flood considerations in CO), recent CO homeowners market issues
- State-specific content required by Colorado DOI — generic homeowners content from PCPER does not satisfy this requirement
- L/H-only producers: Verify with CO DOI whether this requirement applies. If not, L/H producers may launch without CO.INS-CE-HO.
- This is a HARD BLOCK for P/C and Personal Lines producer launches

### Additional specialty courses needed (not launch blockers):

**Annuity CE (4 hrs)** — One-time requirement. Separate course needed.

**LTC (16-hr initial + 5-hr ongoing)** — CO has a higher LTC initial requirement (16 hrs vs. 8 hrs in most states). The initial training also requires some classroom/live hours. LHOPT L4 (2.16 hrs) provides partial credit. A substantial dedicated LTC initial course is needed. Ongoing requirement is 5 hrs per renewal (also higher than most states).

### What this unblocks:

Once CO.INS-CE-HO is built and approved by CO DOI:
- P/C and Personal Lines track can launch
- L/H-only track: May be launchable immediately pending CO DOI confirmation that P/C homeowners requirement does not apply to L/H-only licensees
- Carryover: Up to 12 hrs carry to next renewal period
- CO.INS-CE-HO will need to be reviewed/updated each renewal cycle to reflect current CO homeowners market issues
