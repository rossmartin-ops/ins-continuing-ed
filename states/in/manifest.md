# IN Content Manifest

Tier: 4 (Partially blocked — P/C NFIP flood sellers blocked, L/H producers launchable)

- Ethics: 3 hours minimum (Life/Health producers only — P/C producers EXEMPT from ethics requirement)
- Self-study split: No formal split requirement
- Proctored exam: Not required

- P/C producers NOT selling NFIP: May launch (no recurring state-specific requirement identified)

## ⚠️ MISSING CONTENT — BLOCKED (P/C NFIP Flood Sellers Only)

### What is missing:

**IN.INS-CE-FLOOD — Indiana Flood Insurance CE (3 hrs) — RECURRING EACH RENEWAL**
- Required EVERY renewal cycle for P/C producers who sell NFIP (National Flood Insurance Program) policies in Indiana
- This is unique among states in this program: Indiana requires this course at EVERY renewal, not just the first renewal
- Must cover: NFIP flood insurance products, flood insurance rating, flood policy requirements, Indiana-specific flood considerations
- Indiana requires this to be re-taken each renewal — LMS must not block re-enrollment based on prior completion (unlike other states where one-time courses are locked after completion)
- This is a HARD BLOCK for P/C producers selling NFIP flood policies

### L/H producers: LAUNCHABLE

L/H-only producers have no state-specific recurring requirement beyond standard CE. TX baseline (23.87 hrs) satisfies:
- Ethics: ETH (2.70 hrs) satisfies 3-hr L/H ethics requirement
- Full 24-hr requirement met
- Launch pending IDOI course approval

### P/C producers NOT selling NFIP: LAUNCHABLE

P/C producers who do not sell NFIP flood policies have no state-specific recurring requirement. TX baseline satisfies their 24-hr CE. Note: P/C producers are exempt from the ethics requirement — ETH counts as general CE but is not required.

### Additional specialty courses needed (not launch blockers for non-flood sellers):

**Annuity CE (4 hrs)** — One-time requirement. Separate course needed.

**LTC (8-hr initial)** — For LTC-licensed producers. LHOPT L4 (2.16 hrs) provides partial credit. Dedicated LTC initial supplement needed.

### What this unblocks:

Once IN.INS-CE-FLOOD is built and approved by IDOI:
- P/C NFIP flood sellers can launch the full program
- Important: IN.INS-CE-FLOOD must be re-taken every renewal. LMS configuration for this course must allow repeat enrollment (unlike typical one-time courses). Do NOT apply the standard "already completed" lock to this course in IN.
- Carryover: Up to 12 hrs carry to next renewal period
