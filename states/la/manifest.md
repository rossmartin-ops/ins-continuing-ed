# LA Content Manifest

State: Louisiana
Tier: 2 (TX Baseline — no state-specific content required)
Requirement: 24 CE hours per 2-year license term
- Ethics: 3 hours minimum
- Self-study cap: 12 hrs (50% of total)
- Proctored exam: Not required
- Carryover: Up to 10 hrs to next renewal period
- License renewal cycle: 2 years

Notes: TX selection total self-study = 10.77 hrs, which is under the 12-hr cap — no adjustment needed. Confirm with LDI whether a legislative update course is required for producers; as of this manifest, no such requirement is confirmed mandatory in the CE catalog. ETH course (2.70 hrs) satisfies the 3-hr ethics requirement.

- Carryover: Up to 10 hrs may carry over to next renewal period.
- Legislative update: Confirm with Louisiana Department of Insurance (LDI) whether a state-specific legislative/administrative update course is required each cycle. Not currently confirmed as mandatory in the CE catalog.
- No proctored final exam required.
- No state-specific content required — TX baseline content is launchable as-is pending LDI course approval.
