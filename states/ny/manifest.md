## ⚠️ MISSING CONTENT — BLOCKED

### What is missing:

**NY.INS-CE-LAW — New York Insurance Law (1 hr)**
- Mandatory each renewal cycle for ALL NY-licensed producers
- Must cover New York-specific insurance statutes, NY DFS regulations, and producer conduct requirements under NY law
- State-specific content — cannot be substituted with generic material
- This is a HARD BLOCK: NY program cannot launch without this course

**NY.INS-CE-DIV — Diversity, Inclusion, and Elimination of Bias (1 hr)**
- Mandatory each renewal cycle for ALL NY-licensed producers
- Must cover diversity and inclusion principles, implicit bias, fair treatment of insurance consumers
- NY DFS has specific content requirements for this course
- This is a HARD BLOCK: NY program cannot launch without this course

### Additional specialty courses needed (not launch blockers if the mandatory courses above are built):

**NY Flood CE (1 hr)** — Mandatory each renewal for P/C producers. Separate course needed.

**Enhanced Flood CE (3 hrs)** — Required for NFIP-authorized flood insurance sellers each renewal. Separate course needed.

**NY LTC (6 hrs, state-specific)** — 1-time requirement for LTC-licensed producers. NY requires a state-specific 6-hr LTC course. Separate course needed.

**Reg 187 Training (annuity/life)** — NY Regulation 187 (Best Interest standard) training required for life and annuity producers. NY-specific. Separate course needed.

### What this unblocks:

Once NY.INS-CE-LAW and NY.INS-CE-DIV are built and approved by NY DFS:
- Full NY program can launch
- Single-line track: 15-hr selection from TX baseline + NY mandatory courses
- Dual-line track: 30-hr program = TX baseline (23.87) + NY mandatory (2 hrs) + supplemental content (~4 hrs from excluded TX chapters)
- Course repeat lifetime restriction: LMS must enforce — no producer can repeat any course ever. Platform tracking required.
- CE due 60 days before expiration: Ensure learner communications and enrollment windows reflect this early deadline.
