## SD State-Specific Notes

- Ethics: No ethics requirement in South Dakota. ETH course (2.70 hrs) is included as professional development content — it counts toward the 20-hr total but is not labeled as "ethics hours" in reporting.
- LTC ongoing requirement: 4 hrs per renewal cycle for LTC-licensed producers. LHOPT L4 Ch1-Ch3 = 2.16 hrs of LTC-relevant content. A supplemental LTC course or additional LTC chapters will be needed to satisfy the full 4-hr LTC ongoing requirement.
- Carryover: None. All hours must be earned within the current renewal period.
- Single-line producers: 10-hr requirement. Course registration flow should allow single-line producers to stop at 10 hrs (or enroll in a single-line track). Dual-line track = 20.80 hrs full program.
- No proctored exam required.
- No state-specific content required — TX baseline (trimmed) is launchable pending SD DOI course approval.
