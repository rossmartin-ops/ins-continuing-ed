# TX Content Manifest

State: Texas
Requirement: 24 CE hours per 2-year license term
- Classroom equivalent: minimum 12 hours
- Self-study: maximum 12 hours
- Ethics/consumer protection: minimum 3 hours

Hours calculated using TDI Basic formula: words ÷ 180 ÷ 50 = credit hours
