## VT State-Specific Notes

- Content selection is identical to TX (23.87 hrs meets the 24-hr requirement).
- Both online classroom equivalent and online self-study are recognized by the VT DFR.
- Self-study: Vermont requires a CE Attestation form to be submitted after course completion — this is not a proctored exam requirement, but a post-completion attestation. Learners must be informed to submit this form; the platform should surface this requirement at course completion.
- Flood insurance: 1x 3-hour course required for P&C licensees — not covered by this catalog; must be sourced separately.
- LTC training: 8-hour initial + 4-hour renewal requirement — not covered by this catalog; flag for producers.
- Annuity training: 1x 4-hour course required (once, not recurring) — not covered by this catalog; must be sourced separately or built as a standalone course.
- No carryover information confirmed; verify with VT DFR before launch.
