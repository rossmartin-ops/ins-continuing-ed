- Ethics/consumer protection: minimum 3 hours

Hours calculated using standard formula: words ÷ 180 ÷ 50 = credit hours

## MA State-Specific Notes

- **Coverage gap: This catalog provides ~37.50 hrs. Massachusetts requires 60 hrs (first renewal) or 45 hrs (subsequent renewals). Producers must supplement with 7.5–22.5 additional hours from other approved CE providers.**
- Both online classroom equivalent and online self-study are recognized by the MA DOI.
- Self-study requires a proctored exam in Massachusetts. Platform must support approved online proctoring before MA launch — this is a hard regulatory requirement.
- Up to 45 hours of excess CE may be carried over to the next renewal period in Massachusetts.
- Annuity training: 1x 4-hour course required — not covered by this catalog; must be sourced separately or built as a standalone course.
- LTC training: 8-hour initial + 4-hour renewal requirement — not covered by this catalog; flag for producers.
- Flood insurance: 1x 3-hour course required for P&C licensees — not covered by this catalog; must be sourced separately.
- Additional course development needed to reach the 45–60 hr requirement. Priority topics to build: surplus lines, risk management advanced topics, cyber liability, or state-specific Massachusetts law and regulation content.
