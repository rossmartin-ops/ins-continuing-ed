# Texas Insurance CE Requirements

Source: Texas Department of Insurance (TDI)

## License Term

- 2-year license renewal cycle

## Hour Requirements

| License Type | Total Hours | Classroom Equiv Min | Self-Study Max | Ethics Required |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Life, Health, P&C, Adjusters | 24 hrs | 12 hrs | 12 hrs | 3 hrs |

## Delivery Method Definitions

### Classroom Equivalent

An online/remote course treated like a physical classroom for CE credit.

**Requirements:**
- Forced unit progression (must complete each unit before advancing)
- Embedded interactive checks/quizzes per unit
- Minimum 70% score to pass each unit check
- Content timed to align with approved CE hours
- No final proctored exam required

**Credit hour calculation:** Based on run time of mandatory interactive elements.
See: https://www.tdi.texas.gov/agent/education-providers/credit-hour-calculation.html

### Self-Study

Independent, self-paced study validated by a final exam.

**Requirements:**
- Final exam required (minimum 10 questions per credit hour)
- 70% passing score on final exam
- No forced progression required
- No interactive checkpoints required
- No proctoring required for exam

**Credit hour calculation:** Provider may designate Basic/Intermediate/Advanced difficulty. Hours based on word count.
See: https://www.tdi.texas.gov/agent/education-providers/credit-hour-calculation.html
