# State CE Requirements Matrix

All US states except CA, IL, TX (TX documented separately in research/tx-requirements.md).
Research as of early 2026 — verify with official state insurance department before submitting courses.

Hours shown are for insurance producers/agents (life, health, P&C). Adjuster requirements may differ.

## Gap Analysis

### States that match TX structure (24 hrs, 12 classroom equiv min, 12 self-study max)
Our existing courses can serve these with a manifest only — no new content needed:
- **NJ** (24 hrs, 12/12 split)
- **VT** (24 hrs, 12/12 split)
- **NC** (24 hrs, 12 interactive min)
- **UT** (24 hrs, 12/12 split)

### States with 24 hrs and no classroom/self-study split requirement
Existing courses work but manifest may need hour adjustments. No split constraint — full flexibility on delivery mix:
ME, NH, RI, PA, DE, MD, WV, SC, GA (24 base), AL, MS, TN, KY, AR, OK, OH, IN, MI, WI, MN, ND, NE, WY, MT, ID, WA, AK, HI, SD (10/20 hrs)

### States with non-standard hour totals
Manifests need hour recalculation:
- **MO**: 16 hrs
- **VA**: 16 hrs (single line) / 24 hrs (dual line)
- **NY**: 15 hrs (single) / 30 hrs (dual)
- **KS**: 18 hrs
- **NV**: 30 hrs (3-yr cycle)
- **MA**: 45-60 hrs (3-yr cycle)
- **IA**: 36 hrs (3-yr cycle)
- **AZ**: 48 hrs (4-yr cycle)

### States with mandatory state-specific content (requires content we don't have)
These need new content built before we can launch:
- **CT**: 3-hr CT Insurance Law & Ethics (mandatory)
- **NY**: NY Insurance Law (1 hr) + Diversity/Inclusion/Bias (1 hr) (mandatory)
- **FL**: FL Law & Ethics Update (4 hrs, license-type specific, mandatory each cycle)
- **OK**: 2-hr Legislative Updates (mandatory each cycle)
- **MT**: 1-hr MT statutes/admin rules (mandatory each cycle)
- **OR**: 3-hr OR statutes & admin rules (mandatory each cycle) + 2-hr flood law
- **CO**: 3-hr homeowners CE per renewal (P&C/Personal Lines)
- **WI**: 1-hr WI-specific Medicaid/LTC info (after initial LTC training)
- **MO**: MO law/suitability folded into 3-hr ethics (may be addressed by ethics course)
- **IN**: Flood: 3-hr every renewal (recurring, not one-time — different from other states)

### States with proctored exam requirements for self-study
These require a proctored/monitored online exam for self-study courses. Our self-study courses would need to meet this:
CT, MA, DC, WV, GA, AL, MS, AR, IA, NV, HI

### States with a self-study cap (like TX)
- VT: 12 hrs max
- NJ: 12 hrs max
- NC: 12 hrs max
- UT: 12 hrs max
- GA: 12 hrs max (online counted as self-study)
- LA: 12 hrs max
